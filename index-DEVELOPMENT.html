Our Mission

HackRowing was founded on the principle that the discipline, persistence, and collaborative effort inherent in rowing are directly transferable to the professional world. We offer a distinctive team-building experience where participants learn that peak performance comes not from individual strength, but from the perfect synchronicity of a cohesive team.

What Your Team Will Achieve

Our program is designed for lasting impact on team dynamics, community, and personal growth.

Enhance Team Cohesion

Build stronger connections in a dynamic environment that promotes an active lifestyle and true collaboration.

Promote Rowing in Bulgaria

Increase the accessibility of this amazing sport, encouraging broader participation among the public and youth.

Support Community Development

All proceeds are reinvested into the NSA Rowing Club to improve facilities and support Bulgaria's future athletes.

Your Team's 6-Session Journey

1

Session 1: The Foundation

An on-land introduction to rowing technique using Concept 2 ergometers to build core skills.

2

Session 2: First Splash

The first hands-on session on the water, applying learned techniques in stable, multi-person boats.

3

Session 3: Finding Rhythm

Continued on-water practice focusing on developing team synchronicity and communication.

4

Session 4: Refining Technique

Advanced coaching to improve crew efficiency, power, and speed.

5

Session 5: Race Preparation

Crews practice starts and race pace, solidifying their teamwork under pressure.

6

Session 6: The Finale

A friendly regatta between crews, followed by a closing ceremony with certificates and celebration.
